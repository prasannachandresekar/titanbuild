Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Commercial projects typically take 12-24 months, while residential projects range from 6-18 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide project financing?

While we don't directly provide financing, we work closely with trusted financial partners and can help connect you with financing options that suit your project needs.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire United States with offices in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago. Our team can travel to any location for large-scale projects.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, TitanBuild is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in all states where we operate. We maintain comprehensive liability insurance and worker's compensation coverage.
